● ABOUT SONGVERSE

SongVerse is a community-driven platform where creators and listeners connect. Upload your songs, build playlists, and share your vibe with the world.

We focus on engagement and discovery — curated recommendations, community clubs, and feedback tools that help artists grow together.

Our mission: to make music discovery social, supportive, and effortlessly simple.

Our Vision

To empower creators and fans to turn music into a shared journey — not just a playlist.
